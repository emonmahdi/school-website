About Our School

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.” – Nelson Mandela

📖 Our History

Sunrise Model School was founded in 1995 with the vision of providing quality education in a nurturing environment. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most respected schools in the region, offering modern facilities, well-trained teachers, and a strong academic record.

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic education that empowers students to develop critical thinking, creativity, leadership, and compassion. We aim to create a learning atmosphere where every student feels valued, safe, and inspired.

👁️ Our Vision

Our vision is to build a generation of lifelong learners and responsible citizens who will contribute positively to society. We envision our students excelling not only academically but also morally and socially.

🌟 Core Values

  • 📌 Excellence in Teaching & Learning
  • 📌 Respect & Integrity
  • 📌 Innovation & Creativity
  • 📌 Discipline & Responsibility
  • 📌 Social & Cultural Awareness
